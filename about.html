About

This website was made to provide a break even calculator for buying and selling stocks and/or ETFs on Questrade. Currently the website supports only basic buy and sell transactions. The break even price is calculated using the cost information found on Questrade's website.

In order to find the break even price for a position, the calculator will determine the total cost of conducting a buy AND sell transaction.

Transactions may incur an exchange, or ECN fee. This depends on what type of buy or sell order is being done or considered. When the order is a market order, than ECN fees will occur. If it is a limit order, than no fees occur unless the limit order is a marketable limit order. However, if the quantity of the order is not a multiple of 100, than ECN fees will occur regardless. ECN fees vary depending on the exchange and routes used for the transaction.

Commissions are charged for each transaction whenever a stock is being bought and sold. For ETFs, commission is only charged on the sale. Commissions are decided based on the quantity, with a minimum commission of $4.95, maximum of $9.95. Quantities between 495 and 995 are charged $0.01 per share. Quantites lower are charged $4.95, and quantities higher are charged $9.95.
